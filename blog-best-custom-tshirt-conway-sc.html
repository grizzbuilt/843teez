DTF vs Screen Printing

A lot of people search for the “best custom t-shirt shop” without realizing that the print method matters just as much as the shop.

DTF

  • Best for small orders
  • Full-color designs
  • No minimums
  • Fast turnaround

Screen Printing

  • Best for larger orders
  • Simple 1–2 color art
  • Lower cost per shirt in volume
  • More setup involved
