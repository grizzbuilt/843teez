About 843 Teez

843 Teez isn’t just a shirt shop — it’s a passion project built by Heather and Todd right here in Conway, South Carolina.

Heather brings the heart. As a veteran, a natural leader, and the owner of GrizzBuilt LLC (our parent company), she’s the driving force that keeps everything moving forward. Heather’s vision is what makes 843 Teez more than a business — it’s a place where creativity, community, and hustle come together.

Todd brings the grit.A Desert Storm Vet, affecionatly known locally as “Grizz,” he’s the creative mind who loves turning wild ideas into bold designs. Whether it’s sketching out a custom tee, building out a booth for a local market, or dreaming up the next collection, Todd makes sure every shirt has a little personality stitched into it.

Together, we built 843 Teez as a way to give our community something fun, expressive, and local. We’re not a faceless online print shop — we’re your neighbors, putting in the late nights and early mornings to make sure your shirts are as original as your ideas. From small businesses and bands to family trips and inside jokes, if you’ve got a vision, we’ll help you wear it.

Welcome to 843 Teez — where Heather keeps us sharp, Todd keeps us scrappy, and every design has a story worth showing off.

Heather & Todd, Founders
