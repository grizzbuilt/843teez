About 843 Teez

We’re Conway’s local **custom t-shirt printing** shop, offering **DTF transfers** and **screen printing** across the Grand Strand and Myrtle Beach areas.

**843 Teez** isn’t just a shirt shop — it’s a passion project built by Heather and Todd right here in Conway, South Carolina.

**Heather brings the heart.** As a veteran, a natural leader, she’s the driving force that keeps everything moving forward. Heather’s vision is what makes 843 Teez more than a business — it’s a place where creativity, community, and hustle come together.

**Todd brings the grit.** A Desert Storm Vet affectionately known locally as “Grizz”, he’s the creative mind who loves turning wild ideas into bold designs. Whether it’s sketching out a custom tee, building out a booth for a local market, or dreaming up the next collection, Todd makes sure every shirt has a little personality stitched into it.

Together, we built 843 Teez as a way to give our community something fun, expressive, and local. We’re not a faceless online print shop — we’re your neighbors, putting in the late nights and early mornings to make sure your shirts are as original as your ideas. From small businesses and bands to family trips and inside jokes, if you’ve got a vision, we’ll help you wear it.

Welcome to 843 Teez — where Heather keeps us sharp, Todd keeps us scrappy, and every design has a story worth showing off.

Heather & Todd, Founders

What We Do

At 843 Teez, we believe every shirt tells a story—whether it’s for one person or an entire crowd. Need just a single tee for a special night out? We’ve got you. Planning an event, business launch, or family reunion and need hundreds? We can handle that too. With no minimum order requirements, you have the freedom to print exactly what you need.

Our full-color **DTF transfers** deliver high-detail, vibrant prints that make designs pop, while our classic **screen printing** is bold, durable, and perfect for single-color styles. Already have artwork? Bring us your design and we’ll make sure it prints perfectly. Starting with just an idea? Our team will work with you to sketch, refine, and bring your vision to life. Plus, with fast turnaround times and local service you can count on, you’ll never be left waiting. From first concept to final press, 843 Teez makes custom printing easy, reliable, and built to impress.

**843 Teez** is a veteran-owned custom t-shirt printing shop based in Conway, South Carolina. We specialize in **DTF transfers**, **screen printing**, and **custom apparel** for the **Myrtle Beach**, **Grand Strand**, and **Horry County** areas. Whether you need **one custom shirt** or a full **bulk order**, we print everything locally — no minimums, fast turnaround, and creative designs made by Heather and Todd (“Grizz”).
