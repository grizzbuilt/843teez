🥇 Our Custom Apparel Services: DTF and Screen Printing

At 843 Teez, we believe every shirt tells a story. We offer two powerful printing methods to cover all your needs, from a single gift to massive **bulk orders** in the **Myrtle Beach** and **Conway, SC** area. **No minimum order requirements!**

Direct-to-Film (DTF) Transfers

DTF is ideal for high-detail, vibrant, and multi-color designs. Great for:

  • **No Minimums:** Perfect for single shirts or small business runs.
  • **Full Color & Detail:** Flawless reproduction of complex logos and photographs.
  • **Fast Turnaround:** Quick service for urgent orders in Horry County.

Traditional Screen Printing

Screen printing is the durable, economical choice for larger orders:

  • **Bulk Savings:** Best pricing for orders of 25+ shirts.
  • **High Durability:** Bold, long-lasting prints that withstand heavy wear.
  • **Classic Look:** Ideal for simple, 1 to 2-color team or event apparel.

Need help choosing? Contact our Conway team for expert advice on your custom project!
