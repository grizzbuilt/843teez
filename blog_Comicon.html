Power ComiCon Recap: Bold Tees, Big Fandoms, and Florence Fun!

Posted: September 19, 2025 | By: Heather & Todd

Last weekend, the 843 Teez crew of **Heather & Todd** took our coastal creativity on the road to **Power ComiCon in Florence, South Carolina**! We packed up our boldest, weirdest, and most exclusive designs to share with fans, collectors, and fellow makers at one of the Southeast’s best pop culture events.

What We Brought

We didn’t just bring t-shirts — we brought statements you can wear. From limited-run prints you couldn’t find anywhere else, to designs that mashed up pop culture with that signature 843 flavor, our booth pushed the boundaries of what a con tee could be. Fans loved the fresh, loud, and unforgettable looks.

Why It Mattered

Power ComiCon wasn’t just about comics — it was about community, creativity, and fandom. That’s exactly what 843 Teez is built on. We loved seeing how our designs sparked conversations, laughs, and memories. Being surrounded by cosplay energy and fan passion gave us a whole new wave of inspiration for what’s next.

Exclusive Event Teez

We rolled out **five brand-new, event-exclusive designs** just for Power ComiCon. Once they were gone, they were gone — and they sold fast! Collectors, cosplayers, and fans alike walked away with something truly one-of-a-kind.

Catch Us Next!

If you missed us at Power ComiCon, don’t worry—

Next weekend (Sept 27th and 28th) we'll be in Myrtle Beach, for the **Southeast Punk Flea Market**!
