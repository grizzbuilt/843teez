Shipping Policy

Details on production, shipping times, and options for local delivery in Horry County.

Back to Shop

🚚 Every 843 Teez product is **custom-made just for you** — no dusty warehouse shelves here. Once your order is placed, it goes into production and ships to your doorstep.

⏱ Production Time

All items are printed on demand. Please allow **3–7 business days** for production before your order ships.

📍 Local Delivery (Horry County)

We offer free local delivery for customers located within Horry County, SC.

When you select "Local Pickup" or "Local Delivery" at checkout, we will reach out to you directly (via the phone number or email provided in your order) to arrange a convenient time and location for delivery after production is complete.

🌎 Standard Shipping Times

Once your item leaves the print shop, shipping typically takes:

  • **U.S. Orders:** 2–7 business days
  • **International Orders:** 7–21 business days (depending on location and customs)

These are estimates and may vary during peak seasons or due to carrier delays.

💸 Shipping Costs

Shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on your location and the items in your cart. We don’t inflate shipping — just passing through what it actually costs.

🕵️‍♂️ Tracking Info

You’ll receive a **tracking number** as soon as your order ships. If it’s been more than **7 business days** and you haven’t seen tracking, hit us up at info@843teez.com and we’ll track it down.

🚫 Wrong Address?

**Double-check your address** before checking out. If you catch an error, contact us ASAP. Once it’s in production or shipped, we may not be able to make changes.
