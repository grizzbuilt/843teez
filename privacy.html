This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit or make a purchase from 843teez.com.

1. Personal Information We Collect

When you place an order request through the Site (via the Checkout form), we collect certain information from you, including your name, billing address, shipping address, email address, and phone number. This is referred to as "Order Information."

2. How We Use Your Personal Information

We use the Order Information that we collect generally to fulfill any orders placed through the Site (e.g., communicating with you, arranging for shipping, and providing invoices). Additionally, we use this Order Information to:

  • Communicate with you regarding your order or inquiries.
  • Screen our order requests for potential risk or fraud.
  • When in line with the preferences you have shared with us, provide you with information or advertising relating to our products or services.

3. Cart Data and Local Storage

Our website uses Local Storage (a web technology similar to cookies) to remember the items you add to your shopping cart. This data is entirely stored on your device and is used solely to provide a consistent shopping experience as you move between pages.

The cart data stored in Local Storage does not contain any personally identifiable information until you submit the checkout form.

4. Sharing Your Personal Information

We share your Personal Information with third parties to help us use your Personal Information, as described above:

  • We use Google Analytics to help us understand how our customers use the Site—you can read more about how Google uses your Personal Information here.
  • We may also share your Personal Information to comply with applicable laws and regulations, to respond to a subpoena, search warrant or other lawful request for information we receive, or to otherwise protect our rights.
