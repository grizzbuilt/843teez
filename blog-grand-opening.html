Grand Opening: We're Finally Live in Conway, SC!

Posted: August 1, 2025 | By: Grizz

Hey y’all — we finally did it. 843Teez is live, local, and ready to put some attitude on your chest. We’re proud to be based right here in Conway, South Carolina, slingin’ custom graphic tees inspired by everything we love — like Myrtle Beach madness, redneck river days, inside jokes, and that stubborn coastal sunburn that never quite fades. Whether you’re a Dirty Myrtle lifer or just like your shirts loud, we’ve got something for you.

🎨 What We Make

  • Custom DTF (Direct-to-Film) tees – bold colors, smooth prints, long-lasting.
  • Single-color screen prints – classic look, vintage vibe.
  • Designs that get attention – funny, sarcastic, sometimes questionable.

And if you’ve got an idea? We’ll help bring it to life. That’s what 843Teez is all about.

🎉 Why Now?

Because the world needs more shirts that say what you’re actually thinking. And because Conway deserves a local brand with grit, heart, and a little chaos. We’ve been cooking up these designs for months, and the press is finally hot.
