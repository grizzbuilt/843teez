If you're ordering less than 25 shirts, the question isn't just “how many” — it's which printing method actually makes sense for your order.

The Short Answer

For most small orders in Conway, SC, DTF printing is the best option.

It handles full color, has no minimums, and doesn’t require the setup that screen printing does. That’s why it’s become the go-to for small runs.

Why DTF Wins for Small Orders

No Minimum Orders

You can order one shirt or ten without paying for bulk setup costs.

Full Color Printing

Photos, gradients, and detailed artwork all print clean without extra cost per color.

Fast Turnaround

No screen setup means quicker production, especially for last-minute jobs.

Great for One-Offs

Perfect for birthdays, family shirts, small business runs, or test designs.

When Screen Printing Still Makes Sense

Screen printing isn’t “worse” — it just works better in different situations.

  • 25+ shirts
  • Simple 1–2 color designs
  • Events, teams, or bulk orders
  • When lowest cost per shirt matters most

DTF vs Screen Printing (Simple Breakdown)

DTF

  • Best for small orders
  • Full color designs
  • No setup fees
  • Fast turnaround

Screen Printing

  • Best for bulk orders
  • Simple designs
  • Lower cost per shirt (volume)
  • More setup time

What Most People Get Wrong

A lot of people try to force screen printing on a small order because they think it’s “cheaper.”

But for 5–15 shirts with a detailed design, it usually isn’t. DTF ends up being faster, simpler, and often the better overall value.

Best Option for Small Orders in Conway, SC

If you’re ordering fewer than 25 shirts and want full color, flexibility, and speed — DTF is almost always the right move.

If you’re planning a bigger run or a simple logo design, screen printing may take over.
