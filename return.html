At 843 Teez, your satisfaction with our custom-printed apparel is our priority. Please review our policy carefully, as it addresses both our premade designs and custom orders.

General Return & Exchange Guidelines

We accept returns and exchanges for items purchased from our **premade online collection** within **30 days** of delivery, provided the items meet the following conditions:

  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and undamaged.
  • Original tags must be attached (if applicable).
  • Items must be free of stains, odors, or pet hair.
  • The original purchase receipt or proof of payment must be included.

Policy for Flawed or Incorrect Items

If your apparel arrives damaged, flawed, or incorrect (wrong size/color/design than what was ordered), we sincerely apologize. We will gladly accept a return or exchange at **no extra cost** to you.

Please contact us immediately (within 7 days of delivery) with photos of the defect, and we will expedite a replacement or issue a full refund, including original shipping costs.

Non-Returnable Items (Custom Orders)

Due to the personalized nature of our business, **custom apparel orders** (items created specifically with your design, names, or personalized text) are generally **non-returnable and non-refundable**, except in the case of manufacturing flaws or printing errors on our part.

We urge you to double-check all custom details (spelling, size charts, design proofing) before finalizing your order.

How to Initiate a Return or Exchange

  1. Email us at Sales@843Teez.com with your Order Request Number and the reason for the return/exchange.
  2. Wait for confirmation and a Return Authorization Number (RAN).
  3. Ship the item(s) back to us at the address provided in your RAN email.
