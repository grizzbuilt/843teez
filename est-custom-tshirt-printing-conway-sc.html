🧵 DTF vs Screen Printing — What’s Best?

DTF Printing (Direct-to-Film)

  • No minimum orders
  • Full-color designs
  • Works on cotton, polyester, and blends

Screen Printing

  • Best for bulk orders (25+ shirts)
  • Lower cost per shirt at scale
  • Great for simple 1–2 color designs

If you're not sure which option is right, most local shops (including 843 Teez) can guide you based on your design and quantity.
