What Is DTF Printing?

DTF (Direct-to-Film) printing produces a full-color design on film, then we heat-press it onto your garment. The result is a clean, vivid print with great detail and excellent durability—without the screen setup costs that make small orders expensive.

DTF vs. Screen Printing

Choose DTF if…

  • You need 1–24 shirts
  • Your design has lots of colors or detail
  • You want no-minimum ordering

Choose Screen Printing if…

  • You need 25+ shirts
  • Your design is 1–2 solid colors
  • You want the best bulk price
