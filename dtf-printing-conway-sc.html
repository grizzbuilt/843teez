What Is DTF, and Why Do We Use It?

DTF (Direct-to-Film) is the method we use behind the scenes for many finished custom shirt orders. It lets us create a full-color design, press it onto the garment, and produce a clean, vivid print with sharp detail and excellent durability. For customers, that means better-looking shirts without needing a large minimum order.
