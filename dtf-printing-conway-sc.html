What Is DTF Printing?

DTF (Direct-to-Film) printing is a modern method that produces vibrant, full-color designs with sharp detail, then presses the design onto your garment. It’s one of the best options for custom apparel when you want rich color, fine lines, and a durable print—without the limitations of traditional vinyl.

If you’re searching for “DTF printing near me” in the Conway area, you’re in the right place. We print and press locally, and we’ll help you choose the right garment and placement for the look you want.

Why Choose DTF for Custom Shirts?

No Minimum Orders

Need just one shirt? A couple gifts? A small business run? DTF is perfect because it doesn’t require big batch setup.

Full Color + Fine Detail

Logos, gradients, tiny lettering, photos—DTF can handle complex artwork with clean detail and strong color.

Great on Many Fabrics

DTF works on cotton, poly blends, and more—making it a reliable choice for tees, hoodies, and work shirts.

DTF Printing FAQ

How durable is DTF?

Very durable when properly applied and cared for. We’ll include wash instructions with your order to keep prints looking sharp.

Can you print photos?

Yes—DTF is excellent for photos and full-color artwork, especially compared to basic vinyl.

Is DTF better than screen printing?

For small runs, full color, and fine detail: yes. For large bulk orders with simple art: screen printing is often cheaper.
