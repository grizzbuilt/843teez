How Much Do Custom Shirts Cost in Conway, SC?

Posted: April 10, 2026

Custom Shirt Pricing in Conway, SC

If you're searching for custom shirts in Conway SC, the first question is always the same — how much is this going to cost?

Most custom shirts start around $20, but pricing depends on a few key things.

1. Quantity

More shirts = lower price per shirt.

2. Printing Method

Small orders → DTF printing
Bulk orders → Screen printing

3. Design

Full color costs more than simple designs.

4. Shirt Type

Premium blanks cost more than basic tees.

Real Pricing Examples

  • 1 shirt → ~$20
  • 10 shirts → ~$15 each
  • 50+ shirts → ~$10–12 each

How to Get the Best Price

  • Know your quantity
  • Keep design simple
  • Order early
