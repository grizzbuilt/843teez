What Is a Tee Party?

Heather’s Tee Party is more than just ordering shirts. It is a hands-on shirt-making experience where your group gets to be part of the fun.

We bring the shirt-making setup, a selection of party-ready pre-made designs, and the guidance to help everything go smoothly. Your guests get the fun of creating something without needing any experience.

  • Choose a design
