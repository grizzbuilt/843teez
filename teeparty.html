How the Tee Party Works

We keep the process easy enough to stay fun and structured enough to keep the party moving. That means your guests get the fun of making their own shirt without things turning chaotic.

1. Pick your party details

Let us know your date, group size, age range, location, and what kind of vibe you want. Birthday, girls’ night, family event, youth group, or just because.

2. We prep the design options

We build a set of pre-made design choices that fit the occasion so your guests have fun options without the party getting bogged down.

3. Guests choose, place, and press

Each guest picks a design, chooses their shirt, helps place the artwork, and presses it with our help. No experience needed.

4. Everyone wears it home

The best part is that the activity becomes the takeaway. Guests leave with a custom shirt they were part of making.

We can also help you shape the design selection around the age group, event type, or overall feel you want for the party.
