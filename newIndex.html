Why Work With 843 Teez?

Because you want the shirts to actually turn out right

A lot of people don’t just need a printer. They need someone who will answer questions, give honest guidance, and care whether the final result looks good. That’s the part we take seriously.

Because ordering custom shirts can feel weirdly overwhelming

Shirt color, print method, sizes, placement, deadlines, artwork—it adds up fast. We help make it feel simple and human instead of making you figure it all out alone.

Because small orders matter too

Some shops only care about the big jobs. We’re happy to help with one shirt, ten shirts, or a much larger order. If it matters to you, it matters to us.

Because local still means something

We’re based in Conway and work with people around Myrtle Beach, Carolina Forest, Surfside, Socastee, and across the Grand Strand. You’re dealing with neighbors, not a faceless brand.
